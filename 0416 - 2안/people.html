Bio

Jungwoo Christine Yoon is a Korean-Canadian designer and educator teaching in the post-secondary education since 2011 in Vancouver, NYC, Daegu and Seoul. She is an Associate Professor in the Media Technology Contents Department at Catholic University of Korea. She holds Ph.D. in Communication Design from Hongik University in Seoul, MS, Communication/Packaging Design from Pratt Institute, NYC, and BFA, Industrial Design from ECUAD, Vancouver. Her interest in research lies in visual design of online shopping environment focused on inclusion of elderly people with physical and physiological limitations. Her area of expertise in professional design practice and teaching is in structure-focused beauty and food packaging design. She is the Vice President of the Korean Society of Cartoon and Animations (KOSCAS), a member of the board of directors of the Bucheon International Animation Festival Organizing Committee (BIAF), and a member of the board of directors of the Korea Society of Basic Design & Art (KSBDA).
